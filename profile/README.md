# About Ilmiya

Ilmiya is an EdTech platform that provides next-generation personalized experiences utilizing cutting-edge adaptive learning technology to enhance lives through education.

All learners are entitled to the type of education that recognizes, celebrates, and honors their identities, lived experiences, culture, and beliefs. An education that nurtures brilliance and infinite potential in students. We understand that education especially in underserved and culturally diverse communities is mired with challenges. There is a need to transform the way people learn, and progress in their education. We are set on closing the  achievement gap for the global muslim community.

Our mission is to provide culturally relevant equitable education for muslims globally.

To learn more visit ilmiya.com
